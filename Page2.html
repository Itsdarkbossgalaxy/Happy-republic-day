Happy Republic Day 2025

A Tribute to Our Nation and Heroes

Today, we celebrate the unity, diversity, and freedom of our nation. On this special occasion, we honor the freedom fighters who made our independence and Republic possible.

Mahatma Gandhi

The Father of the Nation, who led India to freedom through non-violence and truth.

Bhagat Singh

A revolutionary who inspired millions with his bravery and sacrifice.

Subhas Chandra Bose

The founder of INA, Netaji's vision was to achieve freedom through strength.

Dr. B.R. Ambedkar

The architect of the Indian Constitution, who worked tirelessly for equality.
